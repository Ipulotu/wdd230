Audience

Target audience

We seek to target Business owners who are between 30-60 and who have a Bachelor's degree or higher. We are also tailoring this site to those how are very familiar with the world of business. Our audience will be accessing the site when on the go or on a work computer. They will most likely access our site during weekday day workhours vias work phones or laptops.

Personas

Person one: Young entrepreneur
I am a 28-year-old entrepreneur with big ideas and a strong motivation. I have completed my undergrad business program and am ready to build my start-up. I came to this site to learn more about the benefits of joining a chamber of commerce. I am on a low budget so I mostly use google suits as well as my laptop for most of my needs. I am motivated by my desire to make my start-up become successful. In order to do that I need more information about this chamber of commerce and other local business.

Person two: Professional Business person
I am a 55-year-old franchise owner with an interest in building a location in Beaumont. I have a BA and a master's in business administration. I have joined several chambers of commerce before and what to see if this one is any good. I normally use my work computer at the office to do research. I want to make sure that my franchise would fit in well Beaumont, maybe even flourish.

Five scenarios

  • Where can I go to build my network for my business?
  • I want to start a business but lack guidance. Can this site help?
  • I looking to get better prices and deals for local goods for my business.
  • Why should I join this Chamber of Commerce? (visibility and credibility.)
  • Where can I find a list of all the local businesses?
